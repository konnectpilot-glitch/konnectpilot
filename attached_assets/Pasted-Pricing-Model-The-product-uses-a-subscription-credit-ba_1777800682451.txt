Pricing Model

The product uses a subscription + credit-based pricing model.

* Users pay a monthly subscription fee based on their selected plan
* Each plan includes a fixed number of monthly AI credits
* Credits are consumed only for AI content generation
* Scheduling, publishing, approval workflow, and connected platform posting do not consume credits

Credit Rules

* 1 AI image post + caption = 1 credit
* 1 text-only post = 0.5 credit
* Regenerate image or caption = 0.5 credit
* 1 AI blog article = configurable, recommended 4 credits
* Scheduling and publishing = free

Supported Platforms in All Plans

* Facebook
* Instagram
* LinkedIn

Approval Modes in All Plans

All plans must include both:

* Auto Approval Mode
* Manual Approval Mode

Pricing Rules & Constraints

* Do not offer unlimited brands
* Do not offer unlimited connected accounts
* All plan limits must be enforced at system level
* Credits reset monthly unless rollover is enabled later
* Trial access must be time-limited to 7 days
* Trial users may receive reduced credits if needed for abuse prevention
