Add an advanced “Analytics & Growth Optimization System” with the following requirements:

* The system must track performance of all published posts across connected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn) using official platform APIs.
* Collect and analyze key metrics such as:
    * Reach / Impressions
    * Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
    * Click-through rate (CTR)
    * Follower growth
    * Best performing post types (image, text, carousel, etc.)
    * Best posting times per platform
* Build a centralized “Analytics Dashboard” where users can:
    * View performance by brand and by platform
    * Compare posts performance
    * Identify top-performing content
    * Track growth trends over time
* Implement AI-driven insights:
    * Suggest best time to post
    * Recommend content types based on performance
    * Suggest improvements in captions, hashtags, and visuals
    * Provide SEO recommendations for blog content (keywords, structure, readability)
* Integrate feedback loop from platform APIs:
    * Use engagement data to refine future AI-generated posts
    * Automatically adjust tone, style, and format based on high-performing posts
    * Improve hashtag strategy based on reach and engagement
* Add “Content Optimization Engine”:
    * AI should learn from analytics data
    * Continuously improve content generation for better reach and engagement
    * Adapt content separately for each platform (platform-specific optimization)
* Include reports and insights:
    * Weekly and monthly performance reports
    * Growth summary per brand
    * Actionable recommendations for improvement
* Ensure scalability and efficiency:
    * Use background jobs/queues for data fetching and processing
    * Handle API rate limits properly
    * Store analytics data efficiently for long-term learning
* The goal is to create a self-improving system where analytics + AI feedback helps generate better content and achieve faster account growth and stronger SEO results over time.
